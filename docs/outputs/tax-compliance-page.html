Tax Compliance by Key Indicator

Analysis of tax compliance behavior across different countries and treatments

Project Overview

This analysis examines tax compliance behavior across different experimental treatments in multiple countries. The study investigates how various factors influence tax compliance decisions and explores demographic variations in compliance rates.

Key Findings

Tax Compliance by Key Indicator

This graph displays tax compliance rates by key indicators, providing insights into variations based on different treatments and demographic factors.

Statistical Highlights

  • Significant treatment effects across different experimental conditions
  • Notable variations in compliance rates between countries
  • Gender-based differences in tax compliance behavior
  • Strong baseline effects in the control condition

Methodology

Technologies Used:

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy)
  • Statistical Analysis (SciPy)
  • Data Visualization (Seaborn, Matplotlib)
