Consumer Insights Analysis

Analysis of customer behavior patterns and preferences

Project Overview

This analysis explores customer behavior patterns across different segments, analyzing purchase frequency, satisfaction levels, and product preferences to derive actionable business insights.

Key Findings

  • Premium customers show significantly higher average spend with clear statistical difference (95% CI)
  • Purchase frequency follows a normal distribution with mean of 12 purchases per year
  • Age shows positive correlation with customer satisfaction
  • Product category distribution shows balanced market presence across segments

Methodology

Analysis conducted using Python, employing statistical methods including confidence intervals for robust segment comparison. Interactive visualizations created using Plotly with emphasis on clear communication of insights and statistical significance.
